Made by James Abitria, Nick Rausch, Nicholas Haugen, and Zach Goodin

This game is a Tower Defense Game

Your goal is to survive the onslaught of enemies that will be spawned and move along the path
You do this by placing towers by placing towers with the mouse left click. The towers will automatically attack the first enemy

When one wave of enemies is done spawning, another will spawn until three waves have been completed. 
If you manage to survive all three waves, you win!

The console screen will close automatically when you win or lose.
